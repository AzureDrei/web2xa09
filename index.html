DoggoTreats

about

DoggoTreats is a high-converting Sales Funnel built in GoHighLevel, designed specifically to showcase premium dog products in a visually engaging and persuasive way. What truly sets DoggoTreats apart is its powerful automation.

automation features

- Instant Purchase Confirmation: Customers receive an immediate confirmation email after purchasing.

- Automated Receipt Email: A professionally formatted receipt is sent directly to the customer’s inbox.

- Internal Team Notification: The DoggoTreats team is alerted in real-time when a purchase is made.

- Sales Pipeline Integration: Purchases are automatically logged in an internal pipeline, allowing the team to track order status and progress seamlessly.
